

# Jamistic - Bilingual Landing Page

## Overview
A modern, professional bilingual landing page for Jamistic - showcasing your booking website development services for Greek small businesses like salons, barbers, and clinics.

---

## Design System
- **Style**: Hybrid design with dark hero/portfolio/footer sections and light body sections
- **Colors**: Indigo (#6366f1) primary, Teal (#14b8a6) accent, Dark (#0f172a) for dark sections, Light (#f8fafc) for light sections
- **Animations**: Smooth scroll, fade-in on scroll effects, hover lift on cards and buttons
- **Responsive**: Mobile-first with hamburger menu on smaller screens
